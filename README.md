# Privacy Policy for PDF Toolkit

**Effective Date: 2026-07-14**

This Privacy Policy applies only to the **PDF Toolkit** mobile application (the “Application”). The Application is operated by **gepsilon** (the “Service Provider”).

## Information Collection and Use

PDF Toolkit does not collect, store, or process any personal information.  
All PDF processing occurs locally on your device. No registration or account creation is required.

If the Application is used while your device has an active internet connection, temporary technical data (such as your device’s IP address) may be transmitted solely to facilitate standard network communication. This data is not logged, stored, or used for tracking or analytics.

## Location Information

The Application does not collect precise or approximate location information.

## Third‑Party Access

The Application does not collect data and does not share information with third parties.  
It does not use analytics services, advertising networks, cloud storage, or external SDKs.

## Opt‑Out Rights

Since no personal information is collected, uninstalling the Application removes it from your device.

If you voluntarily provide information to the Service Provider (for example, by contacting support), you may request deletion of that information by emailing **gepsilon.dev@gmail.com**.

## Children’s Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 16 years of age, or any higher age required by applicable law.  
The Service Provider does not knowingly collect data from children.

Because the Application does not collect personal information through normal use, children’s data is not at risk from using the Application alone.

If you are under 16 and voluntarily provide personal information through other means, parental or guardian consent may be required where permitted by law.

## Security

Since the Application does not collect personal data, the risk of personal data exposure is minimal.  
The Service Provider implements reasonable safeguards to protect any information you may voluntarily provide.

## Data Breach Notification

If a data breach occurs involving information you voluntarily provided, you will be notified as required by applicable law.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
