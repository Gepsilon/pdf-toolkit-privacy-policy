# Privacy Policy for PDF Toolkit
**Effective Date: 2026‑07‑14**

This Privacy Policy applies solely to the PDF Toolkit mobile application (the “Application”). The Application is operated by gepsilon (the “Service Provider”).

---

## Information Collection and Use
PDF Toolkit does **not** collect, store, or process any personal information.  
All PDF processing occurs **entirely offline** and **locally on your device**.

The Application:
- does not connect to the internet  
- does not transmit data to external servers  
- does not use analytics, tracking, or logging  
- does not require registration or account creation  

Because the Application operates fully offline, **no network‑related data (including IP addresses)** is transmitted or processed.

---

## Location Information
The Application does not collect precise or approximate location information.

---

## Third‑Party Access
The Application does not collect data and does not share information with third parties.  
It does not use:
- analytics services  
- advertising networks  
- cloud storage  
- external SDKs  
- third‑party APIs  

---

## Opt‑Out Rights
Since no personal information is collected, uninstalling the Application removes it from your device.

If you voluntarily provide information to the Service Provider (for example, by contacting support), you may request deletion of that information by emailing **gepsilon.dev@gmail.com**.

## Children’s Privacy
The Application is intended for users **18 years of age and older**. It is not directed to children, and the Service Provider does not knowingly collect any personal information from individuals under 18.

Because the Application operates entirely offline and does not collect personal information through normal use, children’s data is not at risk from using the Application alone.

If you are under 18 and voluntarily provide personal information through other means (such as contacting support), parental or guardian consent may be required where permitted by law.
